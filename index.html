How realistic are your mock tests?

+

Our mock tests are designed to replicate the actual NISM exam experience, including question formats, timing, and difficulty levels. We use the same 50-question, 30-minute format as the real exam.

Can I practice without time pressure?

+

Absolutely! We offer both practice tests (no timer) for learning and final mock tests (timed) for exam simulation. This allows you to learn concepts thoroughly before testing under pressure.
